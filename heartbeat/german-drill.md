# German Daily Micro-Drill Template

**Trigger:** Every day at 9:00 AM GMT+8

**Purpose:** Drive efficient German progression toward B1 by Aug 2026 (5 months).

## Process
1. Pick one conversational scenario targeting B1 level (ordering food, asking directions, discussing work, small talk, problem-solving)
2. Write a short dialogue (3-4 exchanges) entirely in German
3. Provide English translation + grammar notes (corrections only, no lectures)
4. Close with a prompt for Herb to respond in German

## Output Format
Under 2 minutes to read/respond. No padding.

**Example:**

---

**Szenario: Du bist im Restaurant**

**Du:** Guten Abend! Ich hätte gerne einen Tisch für zwei Personen.
**Kellner:** Selbstverständlich. Raucher oder Nichtraucher?
**Du:** Nichtraucher, bitte. Und ich hätte gerne einen Platz am Fenster.

**Translation:**
- "I'd like a table for two people" = Ich hätte gerne einen Tisch für zwei Personen
- "Non-smoker" = Nichtraucher
- "By the window" = Am Fenster

**Your turn:** You're at a café. Order coffee and ask about wifi. Go.

---

## Note
Keep it conversational, not textbook. The goal is fluency by Aug 2026, not perfect grammar.
