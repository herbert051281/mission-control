# Domain: travel / Topic: peru_itinerary

The `peru_itinerary` topic provides a comprehensive structural and financial framework for a 11-day family trip to Peru scheduled for August 5–15, 2026. The planning emphasizes altitude acclimatization, child-friendly pacing, and tiered budgetary options.

## Architectural Overview
The itinerary follows a circular flow: **SAL → Lima → Cusco → Valle Sagrado → Machu Picchu → Lima → SAL**. The 10-night stay is divided between Lima (4 nights) and the Cusco/Sacred Valley region (6 nights).

### Core Components & Documentation
*   **Executive Planning**: The `v2_squad_executive_plan.md` introduces a storytelling flow covering high-level agendas, logistics, and risk mitigation.
*   **Detailed Itinerary**: `family_trip_2026.md` documents the daily breakdown, including specific sites like Parque de las Leyendas, Pisac, and Ollantaytambo.
*   **Visual Assets**: A 11-slide presentation deck (`Peru_Itinerario_Estilo_Peru.pptx`) was generated using `PptxGenJS` (script: `scripts/create_peru_estilo_peru.js`), utilizing a thematic palette of Peru Red (C1121F) and Earth Tones (9C6644).

## Constraints and Guidelines (Rules)
*   **Acclimatization**: Arrival day in Cusco must remain low-activity to prevent altitude sickness.
*   **Energy Management**: Daily schedules are limited to a maximum of two activities (Active Morning + Light Afternoon) to manage child fatigue.
*   **Logistical Buffer**: A mandatory buffer (Day 13) is fixed to account for weather, fatigue, or travel delays.
*   **Financial Status**: All figures are referential estimates; specific booking is recommended 8–16 weeks in advance.

## Financial & Logistical Facts
*   **Target Group**: 2 Adults + 1 Child (9 years old).
*   **Budget Tiers**:
    *   **Total Range**: USD 3,750 – 7,050.
    *   **Recommended (Balanced)**: USD 4,900 – 5,900 (Target: $5,300).
*   **Cost Drivers**:
    *   **Flights**: SAL-LIM ($1,150–2,150) and LIM-CUZ ($450–850).
    *   **Machu Picchu**: Entry ($45–62 adults), Train ($120–220), and Bus ($24).
    *   **Lodging**: Airbnb in Miraflores/San Isidro ($70–130/night).
*   **Critical Path**: Machu Picchu circuits and train tickets (Ollantaytambo-Aguas Calientes) require early July reservations.
